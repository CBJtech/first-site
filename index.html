I'm Cherno B, and I'm a developer

I'm Cherno Basiru Jallow nickname CBJ. A tech geek, programmer and researcher studying at Nusrat Senior Secondary School.
I run CBJtech youtube channel, where i share my knowldege on programming, AI and tech in general.
